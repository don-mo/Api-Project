Which Countries are US Exchange Rates Favorable Right Now? Are African Countries All Low-Income?

Overview

This project uses the World Bank and the US Treasury Fiscal Data public APIs to display the exchange rates currently for 1 US dollar to X country's dollars in 2025's September to help people plan for future vacations.

This project also takes an important look at whether Africa's countries are low-income or not. Often we think of Africa as being impoverished, but on the contrary, only 40 percent of Africa is considered low-income and the rest are developing at a current classification by the World Bank that is not considered low-income. We have about 8 nations that are considered upper-middle income (Mexico is upper-middle for comparison) and one nation that is considered high income. Can you try to guess which African country is high-income?
